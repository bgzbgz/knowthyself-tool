Dream Launcher Tool  
Tool explanation and guide
[01/08]
TOOL overview

Unleash the full potential of your life by clearly defining your dream. This tool is your gateway to aligning your deepest passions with your professional goals, setting the stage for a life of fulfillment and extraordinary success.
Type of Tool
Individual
Suggested preparation time
45 minutes
Logical fit 
This tool forms the foundation of the Know Thyself sprint. It helps you articulate your personal dream, which is essential for setting goals that resonate with your true self and align with your aspirations.
[02/08]
TOOL PREVIEW

[03/08]
Purpose
Align your daily actions with your ultimate vision, ensuring every step you take brings you closer to your dream. Completing this tool provides clarity on your life’s purpose, making your path forward both exciting and purposeful.
[04/08]
Step-by-Step Instructions
1. Define Your Dream
Reflect on the guiding questions provided and write down your dream. Be as specific and vivid as possible.
Example of correct entry
"My dream is to lead a global initiative that empowers underprivileged communities through sustainable development."
Example of a common pitfall
Writing a vague dream like "I want to help people," which lacks specificity and actionable direction.
2. Align with Your Values
Cross-check your dream with your core personal values to ensure alignment. This will help you stay true to yourself as you pursue your dream.
Example of correct entry
Aligning a dream of sustainable development with values of environmental stewardship and social equity.
Example of a common pitfall
Overlooking a mismatch between your dream and your values, leading to potential internal conflict later on.
[05/08]

mistakes to avoid
1. Vagueness
Ensure your dream is specific and actionable. Vague dreams lack direction and are harder to pursue. 
2. Value misalignment
Double-check that your dream aligns with your core values to avoid internal conflict. 
[06/08]


f.a.q. and answers

1. What if I’m unsure about my dream?
Start with what excites and motivates you the most. Your dream can evolve as you gain more clarity.
2. What if my dream doesn’t seem achievable right now?
Break it down into smaller, actionable steps. Every journey begins with a single step.
[07/08]
The Science and Logic Behind the Tool

This tool is based on the principle that clear, value-aligned dreams create a strong foundation for motivation and sustained action. By defining your dream, you create a roadmap that guides all your decisions and actions, ensuring they lead to meaningful and fulfilling outcomes.


[08/08]
Final Tips
Consistency
Make sure your dream is consistent with your values and long-term goals.
Clarity
Be as specific as possible to create a clear, actionable vision.
Engagement
Use this tool as a powerful motivator that keeps you focused on what truly matters to you.
