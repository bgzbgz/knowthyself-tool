STRENGTHS AMPLIFIER Tool  
Tool explanation and guide 
[01/08]
TOOL overview

Discover your unique strengths and amplify them to achieve extraordinary results. This tool helps you identify and leverage your natural talents, turning them into powerful assets in your professional journey.
Type of Tool
Individual
Suggested preparation time
30 minutes
Logical fit 
This tool is key in the Know Thyself sprint, enabling you to focus on your natural strengths, which is crucial for maximizing your impact and satisfaction in your role.
[02/08]
TOOL PREVIEW

[03/08]
Purpose
By identifying your core strengths, you can channel your energy into areas where you naturally excel, leading to greater productivity and fulfillment. This tool will help you unlock your full potential by building on your strengths.
[04/08]
Step-by-Step Instructions
1. Identify your strengths
Reflect on the provided prompts to identify your top strengths. Focus on areas where you excel naturally and enjoy working.
Example of correct entry
"Strategic thinking, relationship building, and creative problem-solving."
Example of a common pitfall
Listing skills you think you should have rather than those you genuinely possess and enjoy using.
2. Prioritize and apply
Prioritize your strengths and consider how you can apply them more effectively in your current role. This will help you align your work with what you do best.
Example of correct entry
"Prioritizing strategic thinking in my role as a project manager by focusing on long-term planning and vision."
Example of a common pitfall
Failing to integrate your strengths into your daily tasks, leading to missed opportunities for growth.
[05/08]

mistakes to avoid
1. Overlooking genuine strengths
Don’t ignore strengths just because they come easily to you; these are often the most powerful. 
2. Misalignment
Ensure you’re applying your strengths in a way that benefits your current role and future aspirations. 
[06/08]


f.a.q. and answers

1. How do I know if a strength is truly a strength?
A strength is something you not only excel at but also enjoy and find energizing. It should feel natural and rewarding.
2. What if I have trouble identifying my strengths?
Think about activities where you’ve received positive feedback or tasks you look forward to. These often point to your strengths.
[07/08]
The Science and Logic Behind the Tool
This tool is grounded in the principle that focusing on your strengths leads to higher engagement and performance. By identifying and prioritizing your strengths, you can align your work with what you do best naturally, leading to more impactful results.

[08/08]
Final Tips
Consistency
Regularly revisit your strengths and ensure they align with your evolving role and goals.
Clarity
Be clear about how you can apply your strengths in your current context.
Engagement
Use this tool to stay motivated by working in areas that energize and excite you.
